My Story!

Take a minute (I swear, it's only 1 minute!) to know a bit more about me.

  • Where I'm from

    I was born and raised in Venezuela; and, in case you couldn't tell by my name, I'm half Italian! A few years ago, I decided to move to the US to build a better future for myself, and so my journey began.

  • Welcome to Miami!

    I attended Miami-Dade College where my 4.0 GPA earned me an International Student scholarship at University of Miami. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Creative Advertising and graduated with honors!

  • How I became a Web-Dev

    Always eager to learn new skills, I decided to become a Web Developer. I found out about Ironhack through the Uber Scholarship. After a rigorous application process, I got the FULL scholarship, out of thousands of applicants!

  • The Coding Bootcamp Experience

    I can't say it was easy to balance full-time work, part-time coding classes, homework, living on my own, and taking care of my two lovely dogs. But I did it! I came to Ironhack with zero coding experience, and in the end my web app ended up winning at the graduation Hackshow!
